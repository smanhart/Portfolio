About Me

After working for nearly a decade to help pensioners access their retirement benefits, I have honed a keen eye for efficiency and detail. In addition, I have cultivated the talent to earnestly listen, ensuring people feel heard and feel that their needs are met.

I am passionate about creative pursuits, from designing and building photo booths for parties and conferences, to sewing custom clothes for clients. Moving into web development is a natural progression which marries my talent for detail with my love for making.

Having completed a rigorous full stack development bootcamp with the University of Denver, I have built numerous web applications from scratch using JavaScript, React, MySQL, MongoDB, and other technologies. Armed with these skills, I look forward to building client connections and turning their thoughts into well-built websites.
